### Image Requirements

- **Format:** JPG or PNG (JPG recommended for smaller file size)
- **Dimensions:** Minimum 800x1200px (portrait orientation works best)
- **File Size:** Keep under 500KB for fast loading
- **Quality:** High resolution, well-lit photos of your work

### Tips for Best Results

- Use photos that showcase different styles (e.g., bob, layers, colour)
- Ensure good lighting and clear focus
- Show the final result, not the process
- Portrait orientation fills the space better than landscape
